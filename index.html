Which Character Are You?

Follow the steps below to create your own quiz!

    Step 1: Choose a theme
    Step 2: Click on the "Create your own character quiz now!" button
    Step 3: Fill out template with your questions and answer choices
    Step 4: Click on the Submit button

Step 1: Choose a theme

  • Choose a theme for characters you would like to create your quiz on. This can be any theme you would like!
  • Have a list of the different characters you would like to include in your quiz.
  • Some examples are: Marvel characters, Disney characters, your favorite super heroes, etc.

Step 2: Click on the “Create your own quiz now!” button

  • After you have decided on a theme and picked your list of characters, click on the “Create your own quiz now!” button
  • This will take you to the template where you can begin creating your quiz

Step 3: Fill out the Template

  • Enter your question 1 in the textbox labeled Question 1 and add your answer choices to the buttons under the question textbox
  • Repeat until you have completed the template
  • Enter up to 4 answer choices for each question

Step 4: Submit Quiz

  • After you have completed filling out the quiz template, click the submit button to create your quiz
